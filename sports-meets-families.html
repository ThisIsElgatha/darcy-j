Sports Meets Families

Sports Meets Families Chicago is an annual sports clinic and community fun day.

Our mission is to provide a safe and fun environment where families and youth can come together to get active and celebrate sports of all kinds. While our emphasis is on sports, we also acknowledge the importance of arts & reading. Guests of Sports Meets Families will find fun & interactive activities to fuel the creative spirit as well as the physical body.

Sports Meets Families Chicago promotes the ideals of fellowship, sportsmanship, honesty and respect for authority. The clinic offers a much needed positive influence in today’s society. Evidence and research demonstrates that youth involved in organized sports and activities fare better in school, have improved social skills, are more team-oriented and lead a healthier lifestyle.
